Fun Tips for Kids to Make New Friends!

Click on a tip to learn more about how you can make friends and have fun!

1. Join a Team or Club

Play a sport, join a music group, or be part of an art class. Doing fun things with others helps you make new friends!

2. Be Kind and Say Hi

A big smile and a friendly “Hi!” can make someone feel happy and want to talk to you.

3. Play Outside

Go to the park or playground and join in on games. Playing together is a great way to make friends.

4. Share Your Toys

Sharing toys or games with others shows you're friendly and makes it easy to play together.

5. Try Something New

Join a new activity, like learning to dance or cook! You’ll meet kids who like the same things as you.

6. Be a Good Listener

When your friends talk, listen carefully and ask questions. It shows you care about them!

7. Be Brave and Ask to Play

If you see kids playing, ask politely if you can join. Most of the time, they’ll say yes!
